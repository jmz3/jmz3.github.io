ROS Driver for Fantronic Endoscopic Camera

February 1, 2025 One-minute read
Technical • Guide
ROS • Endoscope • DVRK

ROS1 image acquisition node for Fantronic/Geek Szitman endoscope camera. Supports USB connection. The camera can be purchased from Amazon from this link.

With this ROS driver, you can easily use the endoscopic camera as wrist camera for da Vinci robot, who does not have native wrist camera support.
